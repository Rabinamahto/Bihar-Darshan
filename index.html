Bihar - The Land of Monasteries

Historical Significance

Bihar is a land rich in history and spiritual heritage. Located in eastern India, it is famously known as the Land of Monasteries due to its association with ancient learning centers like Nalanda and Vikramshila.

Spiritual & Cultural Richness

The state is home to several ancient monasteries, temples, and spiritual centers. Places like Bodh Gaya, Rajgir, and Vaishali attract tourists and pilgrims from across the globe.

Non-violence & Freedom

During British rule, Mahatma Gandhi launched the first Satyagraha movement in Champaran, Bihar, against the exploitation of indigo farmers. In the year 2000, the state of Bihar was bifurcated to create the new state of Jharkhand.
